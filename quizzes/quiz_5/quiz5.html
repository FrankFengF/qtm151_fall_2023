(c) Compute growth rates

  • Calculate a new column "growth_euro" which computes the percentage daily
    growth rate of "eur_to_usd".

Hint: Use .diff() and .shift(1) as in Lecture 21

In [ ]:
# Write your own code
currencies["diff_eur_to_usd"] = currencies["eur_to_usd"].diff()
currencies["lag_eur_to_usd"]    = currencies["eur_to_usd"].shift(1)
currencies["growth_euro"] = (currencies["diff_eur_to_usd"]/currencies["lag_eur_to_usd"])* 100
